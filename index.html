Step 1: Tidy Up

Here is a list of some useful tips for winter cleaning before Dec 24:

  • Clear out old junk. Like that ugly Christmas sweater your aunt got you last Christmas - eewww.
  • Rearrange the furniture. Top Tip! Hang your stocking over the fireplace instead of in the fireplace - they won't catch on fire and they'll look much nicer!
  • Set up your own Christmas tree. Decapitating a fir tree and decorating its corpse with lights is a great way to spend the festive season!
  • Do some vacumming. We recommed Dyson, Shark, or your neighbour's cat if you can handle the scratches.
  • Hang lights outside your home. If you choose candles for your garden, turn off your fire alarms - they make this really annoying sound.
